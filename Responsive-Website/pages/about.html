FAQs

How it works

How do I download the app?
To download the Scoot app, you can search “Scoot” in both the App and Google Play stores. An even simpler way to do it would be to click the relevant link at the bottom of this page and you’ll be re-directed to the correct page.
Can I find nearby Scoots?
Yes! The app includes a live map that shows all nearby scooters along with their battery levels. The map updates automatically, so you always see the closest available Scoots.
Do I need a license to ride?
In most cities, no driver’s license is required. You simply need to meet the minimum age, follow local traffic rules, and have a valid payment method added in the app.

Safe driving

Should I wear a helmet
Yes, we strongly recommend wearing a helmet every time you ride. Even if your city doesn’t legally require one, helmets help keep you safe and reduce the risk of injury.
How about the rules & regulations?
Rules vary by city, including speed limits and parking zones. The app highlights restricted areas, so you always know where you can safely and legally ride.
What if I damage my Scoot?
If a scooter gets damaged or stops working, report it through the app. Our team will take care of the issue. Accidental damage may be covered, but intentional misuse can lead to extra charges.
